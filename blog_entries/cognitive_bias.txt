Cognitive Bias
#time 02-24-09 00:30
<p>A few months ago, I was fortunate enough to be asked to give a presentation 
to <a href="http://ignitebaltimore.com/">Ignite Baltimore</a>. At the time, I 
had just discovered the excellent <a
href="http://overcomingbias.com">Overcoming Bias</a>, and I wanted to
try my hand at showing some basic cognitive biases. I used the story
of cholera outbreaks in London in the 1850s, as told by <a 
href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Ghost_Map">The Ghost Map</a>, to try and 
describe a few of the cognitive biases that affect us in our daily lives:
